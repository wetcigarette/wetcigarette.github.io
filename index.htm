Merchandise news!

March 17, 2014

The My Little Pony News blog on Tumblr is reporting that a brushable Lily Blossom figure (and several other lovely ponies!) was found at a Target store in San Francisco! They're apparently not exactly the same as the first editions! The ponies come in simple bags instead of the usual packaging and are much cheaper than the current Lily prices online!

I really hope this is the beginning of a wider release!! Lily is the cutest and best pony and she shouldn't be so rare and expensive!

UPDATE (April 8): I saw someone say that these bagged ponies are now at Walmart so I went to check it out and it's true! Right at the front of the store they have a bunch of cardboard bins and the pony bin has Lilys in it!! But its kind of weird because it doesn't have the pony names anywhere...

There doesn't seem to be many different ponies in these bags. I think it's just the mane six (but not Twilight?) and Lily and Lyra and Cheerilee. I wonder why they keep bringing back Lily since she still hasn't been in the show! First in a McDonalds set and now this! HMMMM......

I guess it's because she's BEST PONY ;)
